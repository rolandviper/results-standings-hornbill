Tournament Formats

FIFA20

Group Stage
All players are placed randomly in group stage with a maximum of 4 players per group Group matches are played in a round robin format All games are a best of 1 (Bo1) in group stage Coin flip to determine home/away if needed All players advance to the playoff stage with seeding Players are seeded in the playoffs based on win-draw-loss record and using goal difference as tiebreakers followed by goals scored
Playoffs
Seeded players will play in a single elimination format All series will be a Best of 3 (Bo3), Grand Finals a Best of 5 (Bo5) If both players 3 games, a penalthy shootout will occur on the 3rd game The loser of the quater finalist, will play an additional series for 5th/6th placing Coin toss for home/away if needed, losing players determine home/away if needed.
