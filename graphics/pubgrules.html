Tournament Formats

PUBGMobile

Group Stage
All teams are placed randomly in group stage with a maximum of 16 teams in a group Group stage are played with a 3 map cycles (Miramar, Erangel, Sanhok) All kills per map scores as 1 point The teams will have score points based on their standings Points for rank standing splits are from 1st to 16th place 1st: 20 points, 2nd: 19 points, 3rd: 18 points, 4th: 17 points, 5th: 16 points, 6th: 15 points 7th: 11 points, 8th: 10 points, 9th: 9 points, 10th: 8 points, 11th: 7 points, 12th: 6 points 13h: 3 points, 14th: 2 points, 15th: 1 points, 16th: 0 points The top 8 placing in each group will advance to the next round
Playoffs
All 16 teams that qualifies for the finals will be in 1 group The finals will be played across 4 map cycles (Miramar, Erangel, Sanhok, Vikendi) The point scoring system is the same as the group stage (Placing points and kill points) Prizepool distribution is from the 1st till 6th place
