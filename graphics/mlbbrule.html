Tournament Formats

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Group Stage
All teams are placed randomly in the group stage with a maximum of 4 teams in a group Groups are played in a GSL tournament format All games are best of 1 (Bo1) For each game, coinflip winner will determine pick order All teams advance to the playoff stage with seeding Teams are seeded in the playoffs based on advance order and time rating with tiebreakers based on team scores
Playoffs
All teams will play a seeded single elimination playoffs All playoff series will be a best of 3 (Bo3), Finals will be a best of 5 (Bo5) teams eliminated in the quater finals will play an additional series for the 5th and 6th placing Losers of each game gets to choose pick order for the next game of the series
