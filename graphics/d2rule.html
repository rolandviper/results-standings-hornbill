Tournament Formats

Dota2

Group Stage
All teams are placed randomly in group stage with a maximum of 4 teams in a group Groups are played in a GSL tournament format All games are a best of 1 in group stage Coin flip to determine the pick/side two(2) teams advance from the group stage to playoffs teams are seeded in the playoffs based on time rating and advancement order with tiebreakers on kills
Playoffs
Seeded Teams will play in a single elimination format Quater Finals is a best of one 5th and 6th placing is a best of one semi finals and finals are best of 3 Losers of each game in a Bo3 gets to choose pick or side the next game
